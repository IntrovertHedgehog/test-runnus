About RunNUS

RunNUS is an annual run organised by NUS Students' Sports Club for the past 15 years. Since its inception in 2007, RunNUS has attracted a wide range of participants, comprising of NUS students, staff and alumni, international students and members of the public. Traditionally, RunNUS offers 3 race categories (3km, 5km and 10km). This year, RunNUS is initiating a brand new segment where participants can form teams of 5 and complete the race together as one.
